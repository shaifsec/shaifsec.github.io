About Me

I am an independent security professional dedicated to helping organizations identify and eliminate vulnerabilities before attackers exploit them.

I specialize in comprehensive security testing across multiple domains to protect your digital infrastructure from evolving threats. My expertise spans across web application security, where I conduct advanced testing to identify OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities, authentication bypass issues, injection attacks, cross-site scripting (XSS), session hijacking, and complex business logic flaws that could be exploited by attackers.

In the realm of API security, I provide thorough assessments of both REST and GraphQL APIs, identifying endpoint enumeration risks, authentication and authorization flaws, token bypass vulnerabilities, rate limiting issues, data exposure problems, and potential API abuse scenarios. I understand that modern applications rely heavily on APIs, and securing them is critical to your overall security posture.

Beyond application-level testing, I conduct comprehensive network security assessments and infrastructure hardening. This includes firewall evasion testing, intrusion detection system bypass evaluation, network segmentation assessment, port scanning, service enumeration, and detailed system hardening recommendations to reduce your attack surface.

My vulnerability assessment services combine both automated scanning and manual verification techniques. I don't just identify vulnerabilitiesâ€”I prioritize them by risk, provide severity ratings, and deliver actionable remediation guidance so you can systematically address security gaps. My penetration testing services simulate real-world attacks to thoroughly test your defensive capabilities, from reconnaissance through exploitation and comprehensive post-breach analysis.

I also provide security hardening services that go beyond testing. Through configuration review and hardening of systems, applications, and infrastructure, I help you strengthen your security posture and significantly reduce your overall attack surface. Every engagement is treated as a strategic partnership where your security directly impacts your business success. With extensive experience in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and security research, I deliver practical, actionable insights tailored to your organization's unique risk profile and threat landscape.
